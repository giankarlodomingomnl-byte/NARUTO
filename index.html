Story — Naruto is set in a world of shinobi who harness a life-force energy called chakra to perform extraordinary techniques. The story follows Naruto Uzumaki, a young orphan from the Hidden Leaf Village who carries the Nine-Tailed Fox spirit sealed within him. Shunned by his village, he vows to become Hokage — the strongest ninja and leader — and earn everyone's respect. Along the way he forms bonds with teammates Sasuke and Sakura, faces enemies shaped by war and hatred, and uncovers the truth behind the ninja world's cycle of violence.

Naruto

Naruto (Japanese: ナルト) is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto. It was serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump from September 1999 to November 2014, compiled into 72 tankōbon volumes. The story follows Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village. The manga was adapted into an anime series by Studio Pierrot, with over 250 million copies sold worldwide.

Story

The story begins with the Nine-Tailed Fox attacking the Hidden Leaf Village. The Fourth Hokage, Minato Namikaze, sacrifices his life to seal the beast inside his newborn son, Naruto. Branded as the fox's vessel, Naruto grows up an outcast. He is placed on Team 7 with Sasuke Uchiha, Sakura Haruno, and their sensei Kakashi Hatake. In the sequel Shippuden, Naruto races to stop the Akatsuki, rescue Sasuke, and confront the cycle of hatred behind the Great Ninja War.

Chakra & Jutsu

Chakra is formed by combining the body's physical and spiritual energies. Shinobi mold chakra to perform jutsu — categorized as Ninjutsu (elemental techniques), Taijutsu (physical combat), and Genjutsu (illusions). Each ninja has an elemental affinity — Fire, Wind, Lightning, Earth, or Water — and the most powerful can awaken unique bloodline abilities called Kekkei Genkai.

The Nine-Tailed Fox & Tailed Beasts

Tailed Beasts are massive chakra entities sealed within shinobi called Jinchūriki. Naruto's relationship with Kurama, the Nine-Tailed Fox, evolves from hostility into one of the series' most powerful partnerships, unlocking a combined mode of immense power. The true nature of the tailed beasts — fragments of the primordial Ten-Tails — lies at the heart of the final arc.
